About me

Arun is a spiritual research professional, however currently working in the IT industry. He has completed his PhD from the School of Computer science and Statistics, Trinity college Dublin. He worked on his thesis titled "Finding diachronic sense changes by unsupervised methods" under the guidance of Dr. Martin Emms during Sep 2013 and Apr 2018.

Towards the end of his PhD tenure, he came back to India and started working with a start-up sayint based out of Hyderabad. He works as computational linguist on conversational analytics. The recorded contact-center conversations are converted to text by an ASR system and text analytics is carried out on those conversational text. He handles the complete NLP stack carried out for text analytics. For his complete list of publications, please look at his google scholar profile
